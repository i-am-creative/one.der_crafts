Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our collection, find what you love, and click the "Order" button — it'll open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. You can also message us directly at +91 9123456789.
Can I customize my order?
Absolutely! Most of our products are fully customizable. Just message us on WhatsApp with your requirements and we'll work together to create exactly what you need.
What are the payment options?
We accept payments via UPI, bank transfer, and other common methods. We'll share payment details when you place your order via WhatsApp.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by product and customization. We'll give you an estimated timeline when you place your order. Custom items typically take 3-7 days.
Do you ship across India?
Yes! We ship across India. Shipping costs and timelines depend on your location. Contact us on WhatsApp for details.
